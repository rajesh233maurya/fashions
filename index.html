OUR PROCESS

Let’s design for you! Fashion designers attempt to design clothes which are functional as well as aesthetically pleasing. They consider who is likely to wear a garment and the situations in which it will be worn. They have a wide range and combinations of materials to work with and a wide range of colors, patterns and styles to choose from. Unusual garments are usually sought for special occasions such as evening wear or party dresses. Today, most clothing is designed for casual and every-day wear. The clothing sector is concerned with all types of clothes, from fashion to uniforms, e-textiles and workwear.

INDUSTRY

Fashion today is a global industry, and most major countries we have a fashion industry. China and Bangladesh are the biggest manufacturers of clothing, and other notable clothing manufacturing countries are Germany, Indonesia, Malaysia, India, Philippines, South Korea, Spain, and Brazil. The "big four" centres of the fashion industry are Paris, Milan, New York City and London.
